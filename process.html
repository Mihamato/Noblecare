The NobleCare Process

From First Contact
to Full Recovery

We manage every aspect of your medical journey so that you and your family can focus on what matters most — your health.

01
Initial Contact
You Reach Out

The process begins the moment you contact us — by phone, WhatsApp, email, or our secure form. You will be assigned a dedicated case manager within 24 hours. We collect your medical records, analyses, and imaging in a fully encrypted and confidential environment.

  • Secure, encrypted intake form
  • Dedicated case manager assigned within 24h
  • Medical file collection (records, imaging, lab results)
  • Initial case review at no charge
  • Strict non-disclosure protocol from day one
02
Medical Review
We Analyse Your Case

Your medical file is reviewed by our network of partner specialists. We assess all treatment options, identify the most appropriate institution, and estimate realistic timelines. You receive expert guidance before any commitment is made.

  • Evaluation by specialist network
  • Confirmation of treatment options
  • Identification of the optimal hospital
  • Estimated timeline & appointment scheduling
  • Second opinion coordination if needed
03
Personalised Proposal
Your Complete Plan

We present you with a comprehensive, fully itemised plan covering the recommended hospital, specialist, treatment pathway, travel schedule, accommodation, and complete cost estimate. Nothing is left to chance.

  • Hospital & specialist recommendation
  • Detailed treatment & care plan
  • Travel & logistics calendar
  • Transparent, itemised cost estimate
  • Revision & adjustment upon request
04
Full Organisation
We Handle Everything

Upon your approval, we manage the entire logistical operation: medical visa, international flights, private transfers, hotel or hospital suite booking, and confirmation of all medical appointments. Your only task is to travel.

  • Medical visa procurement
  • Private or business class flights
  • Airport-to-hospital private transfer
  • 5-star hotel or hospital suite booking
  • Confirmation of all appointments
05
On-Site Accompaniment
We Are With You

From the moment you land, your personal concierge is at your side. We escort you through every appointment, translate every consultation, and manage every administrative detail — so that you and your family experience no friction, only care.

  • Airport welcome & private transfer
  • Certified medical interpreter throughout
  • Hospital check-in & administrative liaison
  • 24h personal assistant on call
  • Post-treatment follow-up & return planning
