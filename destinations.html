We partner exclusively with the highest-ranked institutions in Europe, selected for clinical excellence, research innovation, and experience with international patients.

Paris
France
France — Western Europe

Paris hosts some of the world's most renowned cancer centres and neurological institutes. The French public hospital system — AP-HP — encompasses 39 teaching hospitals, while leading private clinics offer VIP-grade care with rapid access to the country's finest specialists.

Oncology Neurology Cardiology Haematology
  • Institut Gustave Roussy — World-leading cancer centre
  • Hôpital Lariboisière — Neurological excellence
  • Clinique du Louvre — Premium private care
G
Geneva
Switzerland
Switzerland — Central Europe

Geneva is synonymous with world-class private medicine. Home to internationally renowned clinics that specialise in rare diseases, precision oncology, and preventive medicine, the city offers complete discretion and a tradition of excellence in medical hospitality.

Rare Diseases Precision Oncology Preventive Medicine
  • Hôpitaux Universitaires de Genève (HUG)
  • Clinique La Prairie — Longevity & wellness
  • Hirslanden Clinic — Leading private group
L
London
United Kingdom
United Kingdom — Northern Europe

London's medical ecosystem combines world-class NHS teaching hospitals with an extensive network of elite private hospitals. The city excels in complex cardiac surgery, transplant medicine, and cutting-edge clinical trials, attracting patients from across the globe.

Cardiac Surgery Transplant Medicine Clinical Trials
  • The Royal Marsden — Leading cancer hospital
  • King's College Hospital — Complex surgery
  • The London Clinic — Premium private care
B
Berlin
Germany
Germany — Central Europe

Germany leads the world in orthopaedic surgery, advanced diagnostics, and rehabilitation medicine. Berlin's university medical centres combine rigorous academic research with exemplary patient care, offering some of Europe's fastest turnaround times for complex diagnostics.

Orthopaedics Advanced Diagnostics Rehabilitation
  • Charité – Universitätsmedizin Berlin
  • ATOS Orthopaedic Clinic — Joint & spine surgery
  • Vivantes — Comprehensive acute care
